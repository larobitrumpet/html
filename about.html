About

Hello, I am Lucy Robillard. I am genderfae, that is, my gender is fluid and fluctuates, but is never any masculine genders. My pronouns change, so be sure to ask me what my pronouns are, but they/them is always a good default. I also identify as a lesbian oriented aroace, which I am using to mean that I'm aroace (both aromantic and asexual), but also experience significant tertiary attraction (platonic attraction, aesthetic attraction, sensual attraction, alterous attraction, etc.) towards women and other genders that are not binary male. You can also look at my Pronouns.page page to see my pronouns and other preferred words. I graduated from Granite Bay High School in 2020. I earned an Associate in Science for Transfer in Mathematics and an Associate of Science in Natural Science from Sierra College in 2022. I am currently a student at UC Davis where I am pursuing a major in computer science. I enjoy programming and computer science and you will find some of my projects on this website. I am a musician and have been playing trumpet since 2011. I am also a Notary Public in the state of California.
