About

Hello, I am Lucy Robillard. I am a non-binary transgender woman. I use she/they pronouns, that is, I use both she/her/hers and they/them/their pronouns. I am also a lesbian oriented aroace, that is, I am aroace (both aromantic and asexual), but also experience significant tertiary attraction (platonic attraction, aesthetic attraction, sensual attraction, alterous attraction, etc.) towards women and female aligned genders. I graduated from Granite Bay High School in 2020. I earned an Associate in Science for Transfer in Mathematics and an Associate of Science in Natural Science from Sierra College in 2022. I am currently a student at UC Davis where I am persuing a major in computer science. I enjoy programming and computer science and you will find some of my projects on this website. I am a musician and have been playing trumpet since 2011. I am also a Notary Public in the state of California.
