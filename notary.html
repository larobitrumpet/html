Notary Services

I am a Notary Public in the state of California. If you have documents that you need notarized and are in Davis, then email me at larobitrumpet@lucyrobillard.xyz and I can get your documents notarized. If you want to use a ProtonMail account instead, you can email me at larobitrumpet@protonmail.com. The maximum fee allowed by the state of California is $15 per signature, although I can charge less.

I am not an attorney and, therefore, cannot give legal advice about immigration or any other legal matters.
