    • Granite Bay Emerald Brigade 2016 - Through the Dark Forest

      This is the Granite Bay Emerald Brigade performing our 2016 field show "Through the Dark Forest" at James Logan High School. While we did perform at WBA Grand Championship Finals, due to weather conditions, the competition was canceled shortly after we performed. Music includes: Serenada Schizophrana by Danny Elfman, Enjoy The Silence by Eric Whitacre, and Ride.

    • Granite Bay Emerald Brigade 2017 - Flight 19: Lost in the Bermuda Triangle

      This is the Granite Bay Emerald Brigade performing our 2017 award winning field show "Lost in the Bermuda Triangle" at WBA Grand Championship Finals where we won in division 4A. The show is inspired by the real life story of the training mission Flight 19 getting lost in the Bermuda Triangle on December 5, 1945 and was never found. Music includes: Undertow by John Mackey, Lightning Field by John Mackey, and music from Castaway.

    • Granite Bay Emerald Brigade 2018 - Odyssey to a New World

      This is the Granite Bay Emerald Brigade performing our 2018 field show "Odyssey to a New World" at Folsom high school. The show follows the journey of a traveler taking the Transcontinental Railroad west to the "New World". The our season was cut short due to forest fires causing the air quality be deemed unsafe for field shows, and thus we were never able to perform this field show in a WBA circuit. The show we performed at Folsom is unfinished, but it was the last show we were able to perform. Music includes music from New World Symphony by Antonín Dvořák.

    • Granite Bay Emerald Brigade 2019 - Area 51

      This is the Granite Bay Emerald Brigade performing our 2019 field show "Area 51" at WBA Grand Championship Prelims. I would like to point out that the idea and initial design for this show began before the storm area 51 meme. Music includes: Gavorkna Fanfare by Jack Stamp, the X Files theme, M.I.B. Main Theme by Danny Elfman, On the Waterfront Main Title by Leonard Bernstein, Uninvited by Alanis Morissette, Bodysnatchers by Radiohead, and Paranoid Android by Radiohead.
